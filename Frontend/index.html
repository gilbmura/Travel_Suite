About TravelSuite

TravelSuite is proudly working in partnership with Volcano Express, Rwanda's trusted transportation provider. Together, we are revolutionizing bus travel by connecting passengers from Kigali's major stations (Nyabugogo and Remera) to all provinces and sectors across the country.

We are committed to making travel accessible, convenient, and comfortable for all Rwandans. Our partnership with Volcano Express ensures a modern fleet of buses that provides safe and reliable transportation to every corner of the nation.

With our easy-to-use online booking system, you can now reserve your seat from anywhere, anytime, eliminating the need to wait in long queues at the station.
